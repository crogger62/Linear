Representative Customer Requests: Tagging issues with financial levels or risk levels.

Summary

The core concept here is the prioritization of tasks based on their associated financial and risk levels, enabling more strategic decision-making. The main pain point is the challenge of effectively managing workload while minimizing exposure to potential losses. By tagging issues with these levels, teams can focus their efforts on the most critical areas that require attention.
